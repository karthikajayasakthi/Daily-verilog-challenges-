HALF SUBTRACTOR 
DEFINITION :
A half subtractor is a digital logic circuit that performs binary subtraction of two single-bit binary numbers. 
It has two inputs, A and B, and two outputs, DIFFERENCE and BORROW.
EXPRESSION :
Diff= A'B+AB'
Borrow = A'B
