USNG 3*8 DECODER

DEFINITION :
A decoder does the opposite job of an encoder. It is a combinational circuit that converts n lines of input into 2n
lines of output. Let’s take an example of 3-to-8 line decoder.

EXPRESSION :

D0 = X’ Y’ Z’ 

D1 = X’ Y’ Z

D2 = X’ Y Z’

D3 = X’ Y Z

D4 = X Y’ Z’

D5 = X Y’ Z

D6 = X Y Z’

D7 = X Y Z
